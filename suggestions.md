# Scaling Strategy: Unada Multi-Page Roadmap

To transition Unada from a high-impact landing page to a comprehensive B2B platform, focus on these three strategic areas:

### 1. Product Deep-Dives (Feature Verticalization)
Expand the "Solutions" pillars into dedicated pages for **AI Visualization**, **Interactive Sales Suite**, and **Immersive Experiences**. This allows for:
*   **SEO Dominance**: Targeting high-intent keywords specific to each technology.
*   **Technical Depth**: Providing the granular detail, security specs, and integration info that enterprise buyers require before booking a demo.

### 2. Sector-Specific Solutions (Audience Segmentation)
Create landing pages tailored to specific real estate segments, such as **Luxury Residential**, **Master-Planned Communities**, and **Commercial Real Estate**. This ensures:
*   **Relevancy**: Speaking directly to the unique pain points (e.g., "emotional storytelling" for luxury vs. "inventory speed" for master-planned).
*   **Personalized Case Studies**: Displaying only the projects most relevant to that visitor's specific industry.

### 3. The "Proof" Hub (Trust & Thought Leadership)
Launch a combined **Case Studies** and **Proptech Insights** (Blog) section. This serves as the brand's long-term growth engine by:
*   **Validating Claims**: Proving the "3.2x faster sales cycle" with real-world data and testimonials.
*   **Building Authority**: Establishing Unada as a market leader through editorial content on the future of digital real estate sales.
